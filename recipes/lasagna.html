Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Scrumptious lasagna with basil pesto, spinach and plenty of bubbly cheese. Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagne noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 7 ounces basil pesto
  • 30 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring water to boil. Add lasagna and cook for 10 minutes
  3. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated ovevn for 45 to 55 minutes
